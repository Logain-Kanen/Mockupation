Plumbers, Steamfitters, and Pipefitters are jobs that often fall under the umbrella of plumbing. They deal with pipes, drains, heating, and cooling equipment, as well as the tools needed for these jobs. Turning valves, while certainly part of the job, is an oversimplification of everything required for a career centered around plumbing. Monitering key systems of not only pipes but also all the machines and technology connected to them is a critical part of having a job in plumbing.
